What is Designing Clothes?

Designing clothes is the process of generating new clothing by improving or modifying old designs to accomplish new purposes. The clothing design work include selecting the style, color, and materials to satisfy the apparel's practical needs.The art of applying design, aesthetics, garment construction, and natural beauty to clothes and its accessories is known as fashion design. It is impacted by culture and other trends, and it has changed with time and place. A fashion designer makes apparel for consumers, such as dresses, suits, slacks, and skirts, as well as accessories such as shoes and handbags. He or she may specialize in clothes, accessory, or jewelry design, or he or she may work in more than one of these fields.
